About Us
Our Mission
To build great products and even greater engineers — uniting design, engineering, and manufacturing under one roof while mentoring rural youth through practical, real-world experience.
Our Vision
To transform rural India into a hub of innovation, where technology fuels both progress and community upliftment — creating equal opportunities for all.
Our Core Values
People First: We prioritize human creativity and collaboration as the driving force behind technology.
Giving Back: Inspired by Sridhar Vembu, we are committed to uplifting rural communities through meaningful work.
Learning by Doing: We focus on hands-on engineering that bridges theory and practice.
Integrity & Patience: We value honesty and long-term thinking — doing things right over doing them fast.
Empowering Through Technology: We use tech as a tool to solve real-world problems and improve lives.
