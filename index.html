Who We Are?
Pilabz is a product-focused engineering company founded by a visionary entrepreneur passionate about technology and innovation. From first sketch to final product, we build everything from the ground up — while nurturing engineering talent, especially among rural youth. We believe world-class products can be built locally, powered by creativity, hands-on learning, and social responsibility.

About Us
Our Mission
To build great products and even greater engineers — uniting design, engineering, and manufacturing under one roof while mentoring rural youth through practical, real-world experience.
Our Vision
To transform rural India into a hub of innovation, where technology fuels both progress and community upliftment — creating equal opportunities for all.
Our Core Values
People First: We prioritize human creativity and collaboration as the driving force behind technology.
Giving Back: Inspired by Sridhar Vembu, we are committed to uplifting rural communities through meaningful work.
Learning by Doing: We focus on hands-on engineering that bridges theory and practice.
Integrity & Patience: We value honesty and long-term thinking — doing things right over doing them fast.
Empowering Through Technology: We use tech as a tool to solve real-world problems and improve lives.

Kannan Lakshminarayanan

Director
At Pilabz, Kannan brings a career-long commitment to innovation and purposeful engineering. With a strong focus on building cross-functional teams and creating practical, user-focused solutions, he drives the development of cost-effective and high-impact technologies. Currently leading efforts in motor development, his work supports applications across electric vehicles, drones, robotics, and industrial systems. A passionate educator and prolific inventor, Kannan holds multiple patents in currency automation, textile machinery, and mechatronic systems—perfectly aligning with Pilabz's mission to advance electromechanical design through deep research and smart engineering.

Srinivas Tangirala

Head – Computer Aided Engineering Tools
Srinivas Tangirala (Tangi) is a seasoned technology leader with over three decades of experience in the global IT industry, spanning diverse domains and technologies. Currently, Srinivas leads Pilabz in its mission to build a cutting-edge, SaaS-based CAD platform for multi-domain modeling and simulation. Drawing from his strong foundation in Materials Science and Mechanical Engineering, as well as deep expertise in developing advanced 2D/3D engineering visualization tools, he is spearheading the creation of next-generation engineering solutions. His core strengths lie in end-to-end product lifecycle management—including market strategy, product architecture, team leadership, and customer engagement.

Radhakrishnan

Head – Instrumentation Development
With over 35 years of experience in instrumentation, control systems, medical electronics, embedded systems and application software, RK is a seasoned leader in the technology and engineering space. Currently, as the Head of Instrumentation Development at Pilabz, he drives the company’s mission to deliver cutting-edge electromechanical solutions, grounded in rigorous systems understanding, modeling, simulation, and continuous improvement. RK has led technology teams across multiple sectors, from smart farming and irrigation to software products. Prior to Pilabz, he was Chief Technology Officer at a company pioneering smart farming technologies and previously led innovation in a software products firm. A prolific inventor, RK holds five patents in medical technology, including advancements in portable therapy devices, transdermal analyte detection, and active drug delivery systems—solutions that are both innovative and practical. Beyond his professional accomplishments, RK is a passionate HAM radio enthusiast, a certified scuba diver, and enjoys the thrill of paragliding, embracing both adventure and continuous personal growth.
